The world as we know it was taken by a storm which caught us all by surprise and unpreparedness when the COVID-19 pandemic struck. It has been almost ten months since the outbreak was declared as a pandemic and yet there are a number of people even today who have barely any information regarding what happens after you receive the disease. This blog is to help all these people who just want more information about the treatment and who can give better responses than those who've been through this nightmarish disease. We would appreciate any amount of information that you could share from which someone can be benefited.
